Mental Health Support Chatbot

Green Transport Optimization

The goal is to create a platform that suggests eco friendly travel routes and modes of transport to reduce carbon footprints.

This system will aid to reduce environmental impacts and promote sustainability through strategies like smart mobility, electrification, cycling and walking infrastructure, and public transit enhancement.

These solutions aim to balance the growing demand for mobility with the urgent need to address climate change.
